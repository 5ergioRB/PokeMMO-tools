PokéMMO Tips
▾
⚠️
Level Caps
PokéMMO enforces level caps — stay at or below the gym leader's level or EXP stops. Train smart, not just hard.
💊
IVs & EVs
20+ IVs is totally fine for the story. EV training helps but grinding naturally through battles is enough to win comfortably.
🔄
Trade Gengar
Haunter requires a trade to evolve. Ask in PokéMMO global chat or find a reliable trade partner — Gengar is worth it for Lucian.
🛒
GTL Market
PokéMMO's Global Trade Link lets you buy TMs and evolution items. Check GTL for Shiny Stone, TMs, and held items before grinding in-game.
🩺
Heart Scales
You need Heart Scales for Move Reminder — Mamoswine's Ancient Power is essential. Farm Luvdisc (Route 224) or buy from GTL early.
🏆
Stock Up for E4
Bring Max Potions, Full Restores, and Revives into the Elite 4. You face them back-to-back with no Pokémon Center in between.
